VRhythm

The VR drumming simulator

Developed intermittently in a group with 3 other students over the course of a semester as a practical assignment for the "Visualization, Simulation and Interaction - Virtual Reality I" lecture during the fifth semester of my bachelor's course at ETH Zürich, VRhythm is a virtual reality drumming simulator.
The application is split into two main playing modes: the Playground and the Performance.
