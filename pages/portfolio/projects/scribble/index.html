scribble

Unleash your inner artist and guess your way to victory!

Created over a 12-week period from March to May 2019 for a final project for the entire class in the Module 226B course, "Object-oriented implementation" (Implementare orientato oggetti), scribble is a Pictionary-inspired online multiplayer game designed for 2 or more players written in vanilla Java that offers a platform where players can draw and guess words in real-time.
The game is based on a server-client architecture, which communicate by sending UDP datagrams back and forth.
