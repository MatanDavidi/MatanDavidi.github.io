Arduino - Traffic Light

Description

This electronic circuit simulates two traffic lights: one for pedestrians and one for cars.

This is done using an Arduino and two groups of three LEDs: red, yellow, and green, which light up at regular intervals.

Additionally, there is a switch to turn on the green LED for pedestrians only after the car's light has turned red.

The button state (pressed / not pressed) is represented by a blue LED next to it that lights up and stays on as soon as it is pressed.

The car intervals last as follows:

Cars:

Red: 7 seconds,

Yellow: 3 seconds,

Green: 3 seconds,

Yellow: 3 seconds.

Pedestrians:

Red: 7 seconds,

Green: 20 seconds,

Yellow: 10 seconds.

Materials

  • Arduino Mega 2560
  • 2 Red LEDs
  • 2 Yellow LEDs
  • 2 Green LEDs
  • 1 Blue LED
  • 1 Button
  • 7 330 Ω Resistors
  • 1 1 kΩ Resistor
