Arduino - Traffic Light

Description

This program controls a bipolar stepper motor with a Thumb Joystick and an LED bar.

The motor is attached to Arduino's digital pins 8 to 11.

The thumb joystick is connected to analog pins 0 and 1.

The LED bar is connected to digital pins 33, 35, 37, 39, 41, 43, 45, 47, 49, 51, and 53.

The motor rotates in a direction dictated by the thumb joystick, forward = clockwise, backward = counterclockwise.

Materials

  • Arduino Mega 2560
  • 1 LED Bar (10 LEDs, color indifferent)
  • 10 330 Î© Resistors
  • 1 Thumb Joystick
  • 1 Bipolar Stepper Motor 28BY-48
  • opt. 1 Stepper Motor Controller
