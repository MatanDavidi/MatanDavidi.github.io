The Trevano arc (2016 - 2020)

The following projects have been written between 2016 and 2020, either as practical assignments for the various classes, or as work I have investigated out of personal interest. For the complete list, please see my old, sligthly embarassing website.

Name/link Summary
Pittori Small static encyclopedia-style website about painters (IT).
Online calculator Basic, functional but ugly online calculator.
Screensaver You know the idle screen of old DVD players, with the bouncing logo? That, but with big IT company logos.
Text-to-Speech A simple text-to-speech based on the voices that are available by default in your browser.
Tris Tic-Tac-Toe clone with unnecessary login screen, and PvE and PvP capabilities (IT).
Semaforo (ITA / ENG) Arduino-based electronic digital circuit simulating a traffic light on a busy road (IT/EN).
